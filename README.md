## Better Dashboard Overview
### Page 1: Job titles overview.  
![Dashboard page 1](/Images/Better%20Dashboard/Dashboard%20page%201.png)
This page showcases the count of all Data jobs on market in 2024.  
- We can see the star rating of salaries in these fields. We can even see the median yealy and hourly salary in these fields.  
- Line chart displays the trends in data job postings in 2024. 
- The bar chart shows job titles and their counts in 2024.  
- The scatter plot shows which titles have the highest median of hourly and yearly salaries.
- The pivot table displays all these data in table. When clicking on the plus buttonm we can see where were these jobs posted.  

![Dashboard page 1 - filtered](/Images/Better%20Dashboard/Dashboard%20page%201%20-%20filtered.png)
- When clicking on job title, the drill through button in the upper right corner will become unblocked and we can look at the detail of the job title. 

![Dashboard drill through](/Images/Better%20Dashboard/Dashboard%20drill%20through.png)
This page shows detail about particular job title. 
- Gauche charts show the yearly and hourly salaries, their maximal amounts, their minimal amounts and the averages for the filtered job title. 
- Donut charts show percentage proportions of jobs with filtered job title, with ability to work from home, without degree requirement and jobs listing health insurance as one of the benefits. 
- Map chart shows where were the most jobs with filtered job title posted in 2024.
- Bar chart shows where were jobs with filtered job title posted on the internet.
- Tree map chart shows the proportions of schedule types in filtered job title,
